# Product Vision

## Mission

Build an AI business advisor that evolves from idea evaluator into a trusted operating and strategic partner for founders.

## Product Evolution

### Phase A: Idea Validation MVP

Goal: help founders decide **what to test next** before heavy execution.

Core outcomes:
- Assess idea strength and viability signals
- Surface relevant success/failure analogs
- Identify top assumptions and risks
- Recommend practical validation experiments

### Phase B: Operator Assistant

Goal: support early operations once the business is running.

Core outcomes:
- Weekly prioritization guidance
- KPI-aware bottleneck detection
- Pricing and GTM iteration support
- Decision tradeoff analysis

### Phase C: Strategic Advisor

Goal: provide long-term strategic guidance.

Core outcomes:
- Scenario planning and strategic options
- Cross-quarter objective alignment
- Risk alerts and decision memory
- Board-style summary generation

## Guidance Principles for High-Quality Advice

1. **Evidence first**: recommendations must cite cases, benchmarks, or frameworks.
2. **Actionability**: every section should include concrete next actions.
3. **Transparency**: label confidence, assumptions, and unknowns.
4. **Stage fit**: advice must match business maturity and constraints.
5. **Non-generic output**: include context-specific tradeoffs and warning signs.
