# Product Vision

## Mission
Build an AI business advisor that helps founders make better decisions with structured, evidence-backed, and actionable guidance.

## MVP Focus
Start with one job:

> Help early-stage founders evaluate a business idea and decide what to test next.

## Target User
- Early-stage founders (initially SaaS/online businesses)
- Solo founders or small founding teams
- Users who need practical next steps, not generic advice

## MVP Inputs
- Business idea
- Target customer
- Problem statement
- Monetization plan
- Stage and geography
- Team context and constraints

## MVP Outputs
- Idea scorecard
- Comparable success/failure cases
- Key assumptions
- Top risks and warning signs
- Recommended validation experiments
- 30-day action plan
- Confidence level

## Quality Principles
- Structured responses
- Grounding in cases/frameworks
- Transparency on uncertainty
- Actionability over verbosity
