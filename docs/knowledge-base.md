# Knowledge Base

## Objective
Ground advice in repeatable patterns from successful and failed businesses.

## Core Data Sources
- Founder interviews and operator writeups
- Business postmortems and shutdown analyses
- Public case studies
- Earnings calls and investor letters
- Books/podcasts with practical operator guidance

## Case Schema (Minimum)
- Company name and category
- Target customer
- Business model
- Stage and context
- What worked
- What failed
- Major decisions/pivots
- GTM and pricing approach
- Funding context
- Outcome

## Framework Library
- Lean Canvas
- SWOT
- Porter’s Five Forces
- Jobs To Be Done
- TAM/SAM/SOM
- Unit economics
- AARRR funnel
- Product-market-fit signals

## Evaluation Rubric
Score outputs (1–5) across:
- Relevance
- Factual grounding
- Strategic usefulness
- Actionability
- Clarity
- Risk awareness
- Non-genericness
